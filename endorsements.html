The City of Lathrup Village

To Whom It May Concern:

On August 28, 2002 the City of Lathrup Village welcomed the band "Truth" to the City's 2002 summer "Concerts in the Park" series. The groups five members, lead by the founder and leader, Miguel Gutierrez, entertained the audience with a special blend of Latin-jazz and "worldbeat" music. After the concert was concluded, many of the audience members inquired where they would be playing their next "gig".

"Truth" was well received by the audience and was rated among the top two groups performing at the serires. THe group will definitely be invited back for you 2003 summer concert series, which we suspect will draw a large crowd.

On a perfonal note, I would like to say what a pleasure it was to work with the group's leader, Mr. Gutierrez, and would highly recommend them for any local communities "Concert in the Park" series.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact me at 248.557.2600 ext. 225 or by email at info@lathrupvillage.org.

Sincerely,

Jeff Bremer
City Administrator

Miguel Gutierrez is an explosive and dynamic drummer, percussionist, and teacher whose magic touch and ability to move with ease into many different styles will mesmerize the listener. His rhythmic training, musical sensitivity and immense love for his art form shines through each instrument. The owner and founder of Professional Drum and Percussions Studies (www.prodrumstudies.com), Miguel has taught drums and percussion for more than 31 years. He is busy with studio sessions, clinics and performances, and is the leader of the Detroit based world rhythm jazz group 'Truth'. He is also a member of the Percussive Arts Society, has served on the faculty at William Tyndale Christian College, and has been an instructor with Meditation Outreach- instructing the visually impaired.

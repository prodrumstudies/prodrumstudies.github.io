Study and learn about today's drumset. It's the cutting edge of drumset education and performance at its best!

Learn from senior instructor, educator, Miguel Gutierrez who has owned and operated Professional Drum & Percussion Studies located in the Metro-Detroit area since 1982.

Learn to play:

  • Drumset
  • Congas
  • Bongos
  • Timbales
  • African Djembe
  • Many Others

Over 300 percussion videos available to you for your reference and education. Private instruction or group settings are available.

Call or email Miguel for more information and start learning now!

Miguel Gutierrez is an explosive and dynamic drummer, percussionist, and teacher whose magic touch and ability to move with ease into many different styles will mesmerize the listener. His rhythmic training, musical sensitivity and immense love for his art form shines through each instrument. The owner and founder of Professional Drum and Percussions Studies (www.prodrumstudies.com), Miguel has taught drums and percussion for more than 31 years. He is busy with studio sessions, clinics and performances, and is the leader of the Detroit based world rhythm jazz group 'Truth'. He is also a member of the Percussive Arts Society, has served on the faculty at William Tyndale Christian College, and has been an instructor with Meditation Outreach- instructing the visually impaired.
